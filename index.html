Ex Nihilo: Sequentia

Ex Nihilo: Sequentia is a modern port of Ex Nihilo: Creatio for 1.12, which in turn was a fork of Ex Nihilo: Adscensio for 1.10, which was a continuation of Ex Nihilo for 1.7.10.
